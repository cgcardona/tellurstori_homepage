Professional Beat Making Tips

  • ‚Ä¢ Start with Kick & Snare: Build foundation first, add hi-hats and percussion later
  • ‚Ä¢ Vary Velocity: Real drummers hit drums with different strengths - use velocity for realism
  • ‚Ä¢ Add Swing: 10-30% swing adds groove to straight patterns
  • ‚Ä¢ Open Hi-Hats Before Snare: Classic technique for build-up and energy
  • ‚Ä¢ Off-Beat Kicks: Place kicks on off-beats (steps 3, 7, 11, 15) for modern trap/EDM
  • ‚Ä¢ Layer Patterns: Export multiple patterns and layer them on different tracks
  • ‚Ä¢ Study Real Drummers: Listen to actual drummers in your genre for authentic patterns
  • ‚Ä¢ Less is More: Simple patterns often sound better than overly complex ones
