Writing Prompts

Effective Image Prompts

Guidelines for creating great album artwork prompts:

  • ‚úì Subject First: Start with main subject (person, landscape, object)
  • ‚úì Setting/Environment: Where is it? (city, nature, space, abstract)
  • ‚úì Lighting: Time of day, lighting style (sunset, neon, dramatic)
  • ‚úì Mood/Atmosphere: Feeling you want (mysterious, energetic, peaceful)
  • ‚úì Style/Genre: Art style (photorealistic, painting, synthwave, minimalist)
  • ‚úì Colors: Dominant color palette (vibrant, muted, neon, pastel)
  • ‚úì Details: Specific elements you want included
  • ‚úì Keep Focused: 15-40 words works best

Generation Settings

Advanced Options

Fine-tune your image generation:
