Simple workflow to create AI-generated music:

Writing Prompts

How to Write Effective Prompts

Follow these guidelines for best results:

  • ‚úì Be Specific: "Upbeat pop song" vs "Energetic pop song at 128 BPM with synth hooks"
  • ‚úì Include Tempo: Specify BPM for accurate timing (60-200 BPM typical)
  • ‚úì Mention Key: Add musical key (C major, A minor, etc.) for harmonic control
  • ‚úì Describe Instruments: List specific instruments you want to hear
  • ‚úì Set Mood/Energy: Use descriptive words (dark, bright, energetic, calm)
  • ‚úì Reference Genres: Mention genre conventions for authentic results
  • ‚úì Keep It Simple: 10-30 words is ideal, not a novel
  • ‚úì Use Enhance: Let the LLM improve your prompt automatically
