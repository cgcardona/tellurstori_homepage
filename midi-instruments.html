Understanding MIDI

MIDI (Musical Instrument Digital Interface) is a protocol for recording and playing musical notes and performance data:

  • ‚úì Not Audio: MIDI is instructions, not sound waves
  • ‚úì Note Data: Which notes to play, when, and how loud
  • ‚úì Small Files: Much smaller than audio recordings
  • ‚úì Editable: Change notes, timing, instrument after recording
  • ‚úì Flexible: Same MIDI can play different instruments
  • ‚úì Precise: Perfect timing and pitch control
