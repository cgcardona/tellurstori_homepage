Buying NFTs

Purchase Workflow

Complete process for buying music NFTs:

  • 1 Connect Wallet: Ensure Core wallet is connected
  • 2 Browse Marketplace: Find NFT you want to purchase
  • 3 Preview Audio: Click play button to listen
  • 4 Click Buy: Opens purchase confirmation modal
  • 5 Review Details: Price, license type, creator info
  • 6 Confirm Purchase: Approve transaction in wallet
  • 7 Wait for Confirmation: Transaction processes (~1-2 seconds)
  • 8 NFT Received: Appears in "My Collection" view
